Welcome to St Mary Without-the-Walls Handbridge. We are an Anglican parish church in the heart of Handbridge in Chester.

People come to St Mary's from a range of backgrounds, with different gifts and experiences, and we welcome that and rejoice in the diversity of people God calls to follow him. So we seek to be an inclusive community. As we try to keep that tradition of hospitality alive, we look forward to welcoming you.

Whether you are looking for a Sunday service, worship with children, a wedding, baptism, funeral, volunteering opportunity or venue for an activity in our St Mary's Centre. We are here for you.
