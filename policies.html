Safeguarding & Data Protection Safeguarding St Mary Without-the-Walls, Chester takes its duty and obligation to protect all extremely seriously. We have adopted the national Church of England’s robust procedures and guidelines. Read about the policies and procedures at www.churchofengland.org/safeguarding. Data Privacy Notice The GDPR ( General Data Protection Regulation) came into effect on 25 May 2018, replacing the earlier 2008 Data Protection Act and strengthening the earlier requirements to protect personal information held by organisations All registered charities, including parishes, must comply with GDPR to ensure that · personal data is processed lawfully, transparently and fairly · data is used for the specific purpose/s for which is held, and none other without further consent · data will be kept up to date and only for as long as required · data will not be disclosed to third parties without consent of the individual. St . Mary's has drawn up a Data Privacy Notice which sets out in greater detail how we manage and look after personal data held by the PCC and the Clergy team. The Notice is available to view on Noticeboards in Church and in the Parish Centre.
